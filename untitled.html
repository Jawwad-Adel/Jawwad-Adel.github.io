Project Futility

Lead and Camera Setup

The Pi camera is placed directly above a moving conveyor belt where it can detect the passing resistors. The LED creates a more suitable condition for detection. After detection, a signal is sent to the dynamic resistor containers.

Project Futility

Dynamic Resistor Container

The containers which store the sorted resistors is capable of rotary motion using stepper motors. The signal sent by the detection phase instructs the stepper motor on the rotation it must perform for the resistor to drop to the correct wedge. The wedges are also detachable to allow for easier unloading of the sorted resistors.

  • Date: January 2017
  • Client: Explore
  • Category: Graphic Design

Project Futility

Assembly

The final design involves the user manually loading resistors onto the conveyor belt which is powered by a stepper motor these are then sorted into their allocated bins by the identification of the bands using computer vision. The resistors are dropped into one of twelve wedges which will sort for one order of magnitude of the E12 resistor series.

  • Date: January 2017
  • Client: Finish
  • Category: Identity
